Chative is a security & privacy focused chat app.

We, humans, have natural ways to communicate. We say things to each other in private, then we forget over time. Chative is here to do that digitally.

Anonymous

Chative doesn't require personally identifiable information (PII), such as phone number. You can register with an email.

End-to-end Encryption

All your messages and calls are fully encrypted in-transit or at rest. Nobody else, not even the Chative team, can see it.

Confidential Mode

Chative takes your privacy seriously by offering confidential messages that self-delete. Chative permanently erased messages once it is read, leaving no trace behind.

Open Source

We believe secure apps should be transparent in how they work. Welcoming contributions and scrutiny from the community to continually enhance security and performance: https://github.com/chative.
